What's All This Talk About The White Working Class?

Democratic strategists are split on how to address “White Working Class” voters (WWCV, White voters without a 4-year college degree): convince them or ignore them? We think they’re both half-right. WWCV are too conservative to convince and too large to ignore. We think the key is finding overlaps between the issues progressives and WWCV care about, thus convincing some WWCV while also inspiring the progressive base.
